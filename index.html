CodeChef College Chapters are the programming clubs run and maintained by the official chapter leaders and mentored by CodeChef. They aim to make learning competitive programming accessible for students across the globe.

Our Mission

The mission of this chapter is to build a robust Competitive Programming culture on campuses and to help students become better problem solvers fostering learning and professional development. We want to build a community of coders who are willing to help each other.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the structure of the College Chapter?

The chapter structure will consist of mentors (final year), core members( 3rd year), and Executive members(2nd year), and community members.

What will be the role of core members?

It is expected from the core members that they are good in Data Structures and algorithms and can make questions, test cases, and editorials.

What will be the role of executive members?

The executive members will handle the social media pages and promote the chapter. They will be allowed to make questions, test cases, and editorials if they want.

What are the benefits of becoming a Core or Executive member?

Certificate + Swags + Network + Codechef Laddus + Opportunity to be in an awesome club mentored by codechef+ High learning curve for Competitive Programming + and many more benefits...

What is the selection process for Core/Executive members?

Candidates will solely be shortlisted based on google form filled(especially coding profile). Technical and Non-technical skills(Management, designing, etc.) both will be taken into consideration for executive members.
